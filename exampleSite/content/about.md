Hello — I am a placeholder for the About page.

This site is powered by **hugo-theme-splitflap**, a Hugo theme built around a split-flap display landing page, the Ayu color system, and the Monaspace type family.

Replace this content in `content/about.md` with your own introduction.

## What this theme includes

- Animated split-flap board on the landing page (configurable via `hugo.toml`)
- Three color themes: Light, Mirage, Dark — switchable at any time
- Blog section with reading-time, tags, and archive
- Projects index with technology stack badges and repository links
- Links page driven by `data/links.yaml`
- Mermaid diagram support with Ayu-matched colors
- `callout` and `tldr` shortcodes
- Nerd Font icons in navigation

## Getting started

1. Copy `exampleSite/hugo.toml` to your project root and adjust the values.
2. Set your `baseURL`, `title`, `author`, and `params.social` fields.
3. Replace the board messages in `params.board.messages` with your own text.
4. Add content in `content/blog/` and `content/projects/`.
5. Run `hugo server` and open `http://localhost:1313`.
