# Third-Party Licenses

Splitflap bundles and builds upon the following third-party works. Full license
texts are reproduced below. The fonts additionally ship with a copy of the OFL
at `static/fonts/OFL.txt`, as required by the license.

| Component | License | Source |
|---|---|---|
| FlipOff (split-flap JS, adapted) | MIT (per upstream README) | https://github.com/magnum6actual/flipoff |
| Monaspace (fonts, bundled) | SIL Open Font License 1.1 | https://github.com/githubnext/monaspace |
| Nerd Fonts (font patching, `*NF-*.woff2`) | MIT | https://github.com/ryanoasis/nerd-fonts |

## FlipOff

The split-flap board JavaScript in `assets/js/` (Board.js, Tile.js, constants.js,
KeyboardController.js, MessageRotator.js) is adapted from FlipOff by magnum6actual.

(retrieved 2026-06-10):
